About the Temple / ఆలయం గురించి

Located in the peaceful village of Tootipala, Makavarapalem Mandal, Anakapalli District, Andhra Pradesh, this temple of Lord Hanuman with five faces (Pancha Mukha Hanuman) was opened on June 6, 2025. All devotees are welcome to visit, pray, and participate in seva.
